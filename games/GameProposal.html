A Simple Game Because I'm A Simple Person


Use the hippity hoppity button to stay up in the air, like most of your life choices

How long can you evade falling into despair?







(paragraph explanation is below)



























I was thinking a lot about flappy bird and how much it represents my life. On a grander scale, 
I wanted to make the perfect game for procrastinators, made up of mini-games that the players
tell themselves will be the last one. Or that they will log off after achieving a certain score.
The thing is, the games absurdly stupid and not to mention futile. The 'smart' procrastinator will
realize in some metaphysical sense, "ah, yes, I understand what the game is trying to tell me...
let me start my work." The other procrastinator will simply continue, to avoid whatever
it is they're avoiding. This is an example mini-game.
